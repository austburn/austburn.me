Hi! My name is Austin Burnett and I am currently a software developer for the Rackspace Cloud Control Panel. I plan for this blog to be a stream of what I'm currently interested in. At the time of starting this blog, I had an interest in networking and networking security, hence the first post. Aside from networking, I have interests in front-end development, data science, application analytics and Linux system administration. While I may not have substantial experience in these fields, they tend to be where I focus my learning.
